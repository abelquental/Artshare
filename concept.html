program concept

Artshare Festival & Conference aims to create the space for demonstrating concrete solutions based on the latest technological innovations, discussing ideas, discovering opportunities, fostering networking and collaborations among professionals, innovators and creatives who share the same flame to change and creating a future that meets the needs of present and future generations. By bringing together the most innovative, creative and technologically advanced crowd in Europe and beyond, we aim at creating an experience that will bring you the connections, inspirations and solutions to take yourv work to the next level.

In the context of digital transformation, artworks are representations and demonstrations of concrete digital applications. Artistic expressions are essential for the communication of science and technological innovations in order to build a connection and experience with its potential users. Our aim is to bring together professionals, industry and companies who are already implementing and are interested in exploring artistic practices for achieving the high potential for innovation, generating new ideas and opening to new markets.

What can we do now in order to create better hopes for the future?

Hope is the raw material that gives us purpose and sparks determination towards action. In a world of limited natural resources and a lot of uncertainty about the present and the future, pushing technological and innovation boundaries in order to build a sustainable and resilient world is an urgent necessity and a matter of survival.

In the 2022 edition, the event will focus on the topic of sustainability and innovation through artistic practices and the multiple uses of technologies. The event will bring international presence and visibility and open the door for future collaborations. The Artshare Festival & Conference brings together public and private players such as the EC, Research centres, Universities, Tech developers, Industry, Entrepreneurs and Artistic communities, to create a dialogue, generate ideas, accelerate change and foster policy development.

Artshare’s role is to continue globally connecting ideas, people and organizations in a breakthrough and interactive way to inspire them to work and create together. The festival is built on Artshare’s core vision that innovation and breakthrough discoveries happen when diverse topics, experiences and people come together.

The present meets the future.

Which digital innovations can we deliver today?
The future is now! Focus on existing and beyond state-of-the-art solutions and ideas, showcasing how innovation through experimentation and artistic-driven practices can lead to solutions for positive impact. By successfully surviving the present, we create better futures. Activities include hands-on workshops, flash presentations, installations, and performances.


Climate Change

How do we hope to create a critical and creative mass to address environmental and social issues through science, technology, and the arts?
The respect for circularity in nature and digital resources is essential. Topics include Green Economy, Mobility, Water Management, Ocean Preservation, Sustainable Practices in Design, Architecture, and Fashion, and solutions for digitization in these areas.


Resources

How do we hope to digitally transform our world into one tailored to the needs of humans and the planet in a perfect symbiosis?
Training and digital inclusion and upskilling programs. Creative experimentation to support SMEs, Technological Free Zones, and EDIHs. Topics include Next Generation Agriculture, Healthy Food Production, Distribution and Consumption, Manufacturing, Sustainable Building, and solutions for digitization in these areas.


Connectivity

What kind of reliable and transparent connections do we hope to access? General digital awareness and cybersecurity. A new culture of mental flexibility and expansion of knowledge. Axes of terrestrial connectivity: Portugal Porta Atlântica. Civil society and economic and social actors mobilization for digitization and simplification. Topics include Transparency, Trust, Web 3.0, Cybersecurity, Mobility, Media and Communication, and New Media.


Governance

Which public policies do we hope to see implemented supporting our endeavors? Dialogue, engagement, and cooperation between local, national, and international multi-disciplinary actors for developing safe and sustainable public policies. Digital and Green transition, innovation, and talent acquisition programs. Innovative procurement and organization strategies. Simpler and more efficient processes based on proximity to citizens and companies.
